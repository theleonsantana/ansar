What is ANSAR?

Ansar is an Islamic term that literally means “helpers”. In the current events in the Middle East that is our goal to provide information for the refugees escaping Syria using technology.

Ansar Mobile Application

Ansar is a mobile application that lives in the Refugees’ smart-phones. Many refugees escaping from Syria are using their smart-phones as a survival tool to seek asylum in the neighboring countries and event in risk their lives to find safety outside in Europe.

Ansar will provide useful information for them to suvive this trip.

App Overview

Connect with Experts

Our Mission

We are meant to help the refugees by delivering crucial information. Information summited by experts, to Syrians that might migrate outside of their home country and to a safe place.
